About Me

As a Bachelor’s of Science graduate in Music Business and Audio Production, I have a unique blend of skills in music and data science. After interning for artist managers and working at a tech startup in customer support for data-focused projects, I completed a data analytics bootcamp and transitioned into a career in Data Analytics. Although my initial focus was on music-related data or audio, I gained experience in telecommunications and cable entertainment, and later worked as a Data Scientist specializing in Digital Marketing. With a desire to gain more engineering skills, I switched to Data Engineering while pursuing my Master’s Degree in Data Science, where I’m focusing on artificial intelligence and business intelligence. My passion areas include music information retrieval, audio engineering, sports, and data engineering. In the next 5-10 years after my Master’s Degree, I aim to learn more about my passion areas and become a Machine Learning Engineer specializing in audio.

Hobbies & Interests

There is an element of relaxation I love when I get to hang back with my family at home, especially when I have a two year old and two dogs! I consider myself an outdoors-enthusiast, coffee-drinker, hockey fan, music producer, musician, and gamer! I fronted a Metal act by the name of One Way to Live as a head vocalist as well.
